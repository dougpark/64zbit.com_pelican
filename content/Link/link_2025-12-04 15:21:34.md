Title: Crucial is shutting down — because Micron wants to sell its RAM and SSDs to AI companies instead | The Verge
Date: 2025-12-04 15:21:34
Author: Doug
Category: Web
Tags: link, RAM, hardware, 
Summary: Micron is retiring the Crucial brand, marking the end of its line of budget-friendly solid-state drives (SSDs) and RAM kits, as reported earlier by VideoCardz. In an announcement on Wednesday, Micron says winding down its consumer-focused business will “improve supply and support for our larger, strategic customers in faster-growing segments” — a.k.a. AI companies.
